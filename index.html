Miele Licensed Contractor
iRepair Appliances exclusively services Miele appliances. We are a certified Miele licensed contractor — all technicians are Miele factory-trained. Our rate is $150 per hour. Final cost depends on job duration.
One-Time Trip Fee: $150

A flat $150 trip fee is charged at the time of booking. This one-time fee is non-refundable and covers the following:

90-Day Repair Warranty on all completed work
Full Diagnostic of your Miele appliance
Liability Insurance coverage during service
Workmanship Guarantee on all previous repairs

Hourly labour is billed separately at $150/hr based on actual time worked. Your technician will confirm the scope before beginning any repair.

⚠️

Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the $150 trip fee. No exceptions. To cancel or reschedule, please contact us at least 24 hours before your appointment.

01 / Expertise

Factory-Certified Technicians

02 / Reliability

Prompt, Dependable Service

We respect your time. Our streamlined online booking system lets you schedule an appointment in under a minute, and our technicians arrive within the confirmed window — no lengthy waiting periods, no vague time frames. We work efficiently so your household gets back to normal as quickly as possible.

03 / Quality

100% Genuine Miele Parts

We never compromise on components. Every replacement part we use is sourced directly from Miele — not third-party alternatives or generic substitutes. Genuine parts mean guaranteed compatibility, preserved manufacturer warranty where applicable, and the long-term reliability your premium appliance was built to deliver.

Thorough Root-Cause Diagnostics

A surface fix is never good enough. Our technicians perform a comprehensive diagnostic assessment before any repair begins — examining both the presenting fault and underlying system health. This approach eliminates repeat call-outs, ensures the true cause is addressed, and gives you full confidence in the longevity of the repair.

05 / Pricing

Transparent, Honest Pricing

Our rate is straightforward: $150 per hour, billed only for the time your repair actually takes — nothing more. Before any work begins, we clearly communicate what needs to be done and why. No hidden charges, no surprise invoices. Just honest, professional service you can plan around.

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand fully behind our workmanship. If you're not completely satisfied with the outcome of your repair, we will return and make it right — no arguments, no additional charges. This guarantee is not just a policy statement; it is the standard we hold ourselves to on every single job we take on.
